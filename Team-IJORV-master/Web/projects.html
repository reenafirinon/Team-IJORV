UC Teachers Day 2019

UC teacher's day on October 31st was celebrated by the entire freshman class and students in Computer Science, Robotics and AP World History made the trek to Atkinson Hall, the headquarters building of UC.

QI Director Ramesh Rao welcomed students to the UC Auditorium. “Human imagination is our greatest resource,” said Rao, urging students to use their imagination and keep asking questions. The electrical engineer also described a new project his team is working on: a wireless brain modem, part of the NeuroGrain initiative.
